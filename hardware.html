Motherboard

The motherboard, what is a motherboard? The motherboard is circuit board that contains multiple components of a computer or other devices that has connectors for other devices or circuit boards that can be slotted in. The motherboard is the reason why computers work and the other circuit boards are able to communicate to make the computer function properly.

CPU

CPU, central processing unit, is the most important part in almost every computer, but why? The CPU is the most important part because it is the part that sends signals to the other parts of the computer, almost like how the brain functions. The CPU is an electronic machine that works on a list of things to do, called 'instructions'.

Again, you may have read it before, and it is from the CPU, this device sends all the signals to the motherboard and the motherboard sends it to teh part the signals are trying to go.
